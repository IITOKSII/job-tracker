# Capabal.app PRD

## Mission
Empowering people with disabilities through independent career management tools.

## Pillars
1. Universal Data Harvesting
2. Modular Accessibility
3. Individual Agency

## Advocacy & Transparency
- **A11y Ratings:** Users can log and track employer accessibility hurdles.
- **Self-Advocacy:** Storage for accommodation request templates.
- **Barrier Logs:** Tracking technical debt on external job sites.
