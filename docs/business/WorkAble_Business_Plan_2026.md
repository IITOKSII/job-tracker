WORKABLE SAAS BUSINESS PLAN 2026

SECTION 1: EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Business Overview
WorkAble is an Australian-based, accessibility-first career empowerment platform. It leverages generative artificial intelligence to remove the systemic barriers faced by job seekers with disabilities. The platform centralizes job discovery, document tailoring, and interview preparation within a strictly WCAG-compliant environment. By prioritizing universal design, WorkAble ensures that users with diverse physical and cognitive needs can navigate the recruitment lifecycle with total independence.

Goals
The primary objective is to achieve ten thousand successful job placements within the first twenty-four months of Australian operations. Key milestones include securing formal partnerships with three major National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) registered providers and establishing a baseline for user advocacy through the implementation of barrier-log tracking.

Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
WorkAble is the only career platform that combines high-level AI document generation with native accessibility tools and self-advocacy templates. While competitors provide generic AI writing, WorkAble offers specialized accommodation request generators and a dyslexia-responsive interface. It is a tool designed for the candidate, not the recruiter, shifting the power dynamic in the hiring process toward the individual.


SECTION 2: PROBLEM AND SOLUTION (THE PRODUCT)

The Problem
Job seekers with disabilities in Australia encounter two primary hurdles: digital inaccessibility and systemic bias. Most major job boards are not optimized for screen readers or alternative input devices, and the cognitive load of tailoring applications leads to higher rates of burnout. Furthermore, many candidates lack the resources to confidently navigate requests for workplace adjustments during the interview phase.

Why Current Solutions Fail
Generic platforms often utilize dense layouts and non-standard interface elements that frustrate assistive technologies. Automated tracking systems (ATS) often penalize non-standard employment histories without considering the context of disability. Existing accessibility overlays are frequently ineffective and do not address the need for tailored content that advocates for the user's specific requirements.

How WorkAble Solves It Better
WorkAble provides a distraction-free UI that adapts to the user’s sensory preferences including contrast, font, and scaling. The AI engine parses job descriptions and cross-references them with the user’s skills to generate high-quality, tailored resumes and cover letters. Integrated voice-to-text and text-to-speech capabilities ensure the platform is usable by those with diverse mobility and visual needs.


SECTION 3: MARKET ANALYSIS AND TARGET AUDIENCE

Target Users
The primary audience consists of Australians living with a disability who are currently underemployed or seeking career advancement. This includes neurodivergent individuals (ADHD, Autism) requiring structured workflows, individuals with sensory disabilities (Vision or Hearing impairment) requiring high-contrast UI, and individuals with physical disabilities requiring voice-controlled navigation.

SECTION 5: MARKETING AND SALES STRATEGY (GO-TO-MARKET)

User Acquisition Channels
WorkAble will focus on community-led growth and partnerships with disability advocacy groups. Strategic SEO targeting Australian-specific job search terms related to accessibility will drive organic traffic.

Partnerships
Key partnerships will be sought with organizations such as the Australian Network on Disability and various University disability support centers. These partnerships will provide a direct pipeline to graduating students and mid-career professionals.

Growth Strategy
A referral program will offer premium feature extensions for users who successfully refer peers, leveraging the existing network effects within the Australian disability community.


SECTION 6: PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT AND MVP PLAN

Core MVP Features
Automated job scraping and manual input modules.
AI-driven resume and multi-variant cover letter generators.
Accessibility toolbar including Dyslexia font, scaling, and high contrast.
Voice-to-text and basic interview question generator.
Professional document editor with PDF and DOCX export functionality.

What Is Excluded
Integrated video interview hosting (Post-MVP).
Direct API application to external boards (Technical risk).
Native iOS/Android apps (Web-first strategy for maximum screen-reader stability).

Development Phases
Phase 1: UI/UX Accessibility Audit and Core AI Logic.
Phase 2: Template Library Expansion and Accommodation Generator.
Phase 3: B2B Dashboard and Agency Reporting Tools.
